My Vision and Goal

My vision and goal throughot the project was to create a way to help lower the cost of organized sports for families that can't afford them. I created a model shoe desing that can be used for multiple different sports for the price of one shoe. This would help families save up to around 50% on equipment per sport.

How does the shoe work?

For the shoe it is broken down into a clip in mechanism. For the shoe there is a base model. The base model will be used for each sport played. Then when chnaging to go play another sport you can simply unclip the tread and soul of the bottom f your shoe and replace it with the tread of the next sport your playing.
